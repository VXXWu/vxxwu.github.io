ABOUT THE INTERACTIVE MAP

Social scientistsHIGHLIGHT MORE deal with a lot of geographic information is difficult to intuitively understand, but by utilizing visualization tools like interactive maps, geographic data can be examined in an easily processable format to develop new research questions and see relationships.

This interactive map can display a large dataset of 30 different basic social demographic metrics, like gender and violent crime rate, that I gathered from various reliable sources, like the US Census Bureau HYPERLINK(https://www.census.gov/) and the FBI UCR (https://www.fbi.gov/services/cjis/ucr/publications), listed below and can additionally display part of a professionally gathered dataset on crowdfunding. Enjoy!
