Visualization has always been an important tool in conveying ideas and deepening our understanding of different topics by looking at concepts through a visual medium.

One group of people in particular that can take good advantage of visualization in the form of interactive maps are social scientists.

Interactive maps can be used to more easily see correlations and relationships between different variables than through traditional, non-visualized mediums, and multiple researchers can easily use the same data visualizations to study.
